The demo is practical and unlimited in actions. You can type, erase, add, change, and re-test any data. The only limit is the session time for one attempt.

Demo setup

Step-by-step instructions

  1. Choose the country and interface language.
  2. Choose Standard or Team.
  3. Press Start Demo.
