MedControl

Know for sure when medication is taken.

MedControl helps you organize daily medication routines in two simple modes:

  • Standard — simple self-control
  • Team — coordination for several patients and caregivers

Who is it for?

  • Families
  • Caregivers
  • Small care teams

What it does

  • Medication schedule
  • Taken / Missed tracking
  • History
  • Team coordination
